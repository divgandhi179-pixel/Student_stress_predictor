Our Mission & Origins

The modern school system places a lot of heavy burdens on young adults. We created the Student Stress Predictor because we witnessed firsthand how silent burnout, lack of sleep, and immense academic pressure can severely hurt a student's happiness and grades before anyone steps in to help.

Instead of relying on outdated survey methods that nobody wants to fill out, we made a fun, easy, and continuous tracking system. By simply entering how much you sleep, how much you study, and your daily social activity, NeuroMind gives you real-time warnings and gentle suggestions. Our ultimate goal is to save students before they hit their breaking point.

We want to foster a world where mental health is treated with the same importance as physical health. By creating healthy habits and tracking how we spend our time, we believe the entire student community can thrive together.
