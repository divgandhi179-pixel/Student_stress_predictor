Our Mission & Origins

The modern academic system places unprecedented cognitive burdens on young adults. We created the Student Stress Predictor because we witnessed firsthand how silent burnout, erratic sleep cycles, and immense academic pressure can severely fracture student potential before any traditional intervention occurs.

By departing from outdated, isolated surveys and instead utilizing continuous, non-invasive behavioral telemetry—analyzing sleep algorithms, study hours, and social activity coefficients—NeuroMind generates a real-time risk profile designed to save students before they hit their breaking point.
